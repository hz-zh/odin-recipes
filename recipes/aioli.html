Recipe for Garlic Aioli

Description

Aioli can be used as a dip, sauce or dressing! It's creamy, flavorful & quick to make!

  • PREP TIME: 5 mins
  • COOK TIME: 0 mins
  • REFRIGERATE TIME: 30 mins
  • TOTAL TIME: 35 mins

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients in a small bowl to combine.
  2. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving.
